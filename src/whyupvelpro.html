Why UPVEL PRO

Upvel originated from the basis of 20 years networking experience with office and factory located in New Taipei City, Taiwan. As a leading manufacturer, we are devoted to design and produce industrial networking devices for harsh environments and critical infrastructure with highly demanding quality which is demonstrated by our rigid quality control, reliability tests, and certification.

Focus on flexibility and mobility service, Upvel is consistently dedicated to providing total product solutions from Fast Ethernet to 10 Gigabit, from Layer 2 to Layer 3 and from Wired and Wireless to satisfy worldwide requirements in vertical markets of transportation, railway, automation, surveillance, and power utility to help build smart cities in the world.

Advantages

  • In-house hardware, software design team with our own source codes.
  • Specialize in designing hardened Ethernet devices with superior reliability, redundant power support, wide operating temperature range, and long MTBF.
  • Extensive knowledge in internetworking, protocols, and interoperability with products from leading communication manufacturers in the world.
  • Service our customers with quality products, flexibility, quick time-to-market, and exceptional production service

Efficient Operation and Support

  • Effective inventory plan to guarantee the best delivery lead time.
  • Flexible production process to prepare shipment with minimum MOQ.
  • Professional technical support team with know-how and hands-on experience working with solution providers.
  • Experienced team with abundant OEM/ODM experience with global networking equipment providers.

Manufacturing & Quality Assurance

  • All of Konten's industrial networking products are produced and tested in certificated labs and manufacturing facilities, in order to meet the standards in ruggedness and reliability for mission-critical network applications.
  • We understand how quality is important to your business and ours. Each step of the design process from the component selection to design verification testing is closely monitored in order to meet industrial standards.
  • All tests in the manufacturing process are designed and implemented to ensure our products meet the demanding requirements of the applications and the markets we serve.
